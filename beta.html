Omnomagon Public Beta

Download: Omnomagon.apk

If you find bugs or have a feature request, feel free to either submit them to the Issues page on github or write a mail.

Installation:

  1. Enable "allow install from non-market sources" on your android (normaly somewhere in the developer settings).
  2. Download the .apk (either by typing the url in the browser on your smartphone or by scanning the QR code).
  3. Install the App.
